About HumanGPT

HumanGPT is an advanced AI Discord bot designed to interact naturally like a human. Unlike other bots, HumanGPT has free will - it can randomly join conversations, making interactions feel more organic and engaging.

It offers a wide range of features including image generation (completely free!), comprehensive moderation tools, and server management capabilities. While it excels at moderation, HumanGPT is primarily used as a versatile AI companion that brings your server to life.

Always active and ready to assist, HumanGPT ensures your server is both well-managed and entertaining.
