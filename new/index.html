Faisal Masjid - First Stop

Our day began at the magnificent Faisal Masjid, where we experienced the serene atmosphere and breathtaking architecture. Despite the cold morning and freezing feet from going barefoot, the beauty of this place made it all worthwhile.

Favorite Moment: Breathing in the fresh, pollution-free air and taking group photos with friends against the stunning backdrop of the mosque and surrounding mountains.

Lok Virsa Museum - Cultural Journey

Next, we explored the Lok Virsa Museum, immersing ourselves in the rich history and cultural heritage of Pakistan. The artifacts from Mohenjo-daro, Kazakhstan, and the subcontinent were fascinating.

Favorite Moment: Seeing the ancient tools and statues up close, which made history feel alive and tangible.

Science Museum - Discovery Zone

As a science enthusiast, this was one of the highlights of my trip. Seeing real elements from the periodic table, learning about Earth's layers, and examining animal skeletons was an incredible experience.

Favorite Moment: Physically seeing elements I'd only read about in chemistry books - it made learning come alive!

Ayub Park - Relaxation Time

After hours of exploration, Ayub Park provided the perfect setting to relax, recharge, and enjoy delicious food with classmates. The peaceful environment helped us unwind and share stories about our experiences.

Favorite Moment: Sharing food with friends while laughing and talking about our favorite parts of the trip so far.

Joy Land - Adventure Finale

We ended our day with thrilling rides and adventures at Joy Land. The adrenaline rush from the upside-down ride and the fun of bumper cars created unforgettable memories with classmates.

Favorite Moment: The terrifying but exhilarating upside-down ride that had everyone screaming and laughing!

Faisal Masjid
First Stop • 8:30 AM • Spiritual Experience

Faisal Masjid was our first stop. It was really cold there because of the surrounding mountains and trees. We had to go barefoot inside the mosque, and my feet were freezing! However, the view was extremely beautiful, and I loved the peaceful atmosphere.

The air felt very fresh because there was no pollution, and breathing there felt amazing. I took pictures with my friends, walked around, and also visited the mosque from inside. We took group photos, and it was a lot of fun. I enjoyed Faisal Masjid the most.

Lok Virsa Museum
Second Stop • 10:00 AM • Cultural Exploration

After Faisal Masjid, we went to Lok Virsa Museum. There, we saw many historical items, which were very interesting. I saw statues of emperors, ancient tools, and historical objects from Mohenjo-daro, Kazakhstan, and the subcontinent.

Lok Virsa is a very informative and interesting place, and all my friends enjoyed it.

Science Museum
Third Stop • 11:30 AM • Scientific Discovery

After Lok Virsa, we visited the Science Museum. This was one of the best places I have ever visited. I physically saw many elements from the periodic table, and since I love chemistry, seeing them in real life felt amazing.

I also learned a lot about wild animals, aquatic animals, layers of the Earth, fossil fuels, and elements. I saw skeletons of different animals as well. It was truly an awesome experience.

Joy Land
Final Stop • 3:00 PM • Adventure & Fun

After Ayub Park, we went to Joy Land. This place was full of adventures. I, along with some of my classmates, bought a band that allowed us to enjoy all the rides freely.
